Unwinding from Stress

Walking Dancing Yoga
Daily or weekly timed walks are beneficial in assisting to relieve stress. Walking with your back straight and letting your arms naturally swing as you walk are some ways to ensure that you allow your body to distress. Over time, one can increase their distance of walking or work on increasing the speed of their walk. Dancing can be a great form of exercise. It can also be fun and silly allowing you to move your body but also make you laugh. This is a great way to relieve you stress. Yoga is a form of relaxation that helps to calm the body, mind, and control breathing. This form of exercising is peaceful when performed in a space that is free of distractions. You can choose to position yourself in any way that suits your comfort. Set a timer for each position and work through taking deep breaths in and out.
